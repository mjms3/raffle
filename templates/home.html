How will the raffle work?

Gift Donation

You can donate gifts by uploading images of those gifts. The uploaded images will automatically be downsized to 600 pixels wide and a pixelated version of the image will be created. You can review and manage your donations here, including be able to reupload images if you are unhappy with the downsized or pixelated image. You will not be able to change the uploaded gifts once the raffle has started.

Ticket Purchase

You can purchase tickets here. Each ticket costs £5. Exact details for how the gift matching and charitable donations will work will be following shortly but, rest assured, Cboe will be matching any donations. You will also be able to change the number of tickets you've purchased right up to the start of the raffle.

The Raffle

The raffle will be held virtually via this site, so make sure that you're logged in and have the raffle page up ready for the Christmas Party. Please note that the site and raffle format will not work on a small (eg mobile or tablet) screen.

After the raffle

Obviously, it's a little tricky to get gifts to people at the moment. After the raffle, everyone who has donated gifts will be supplied with a list of who has won those gifts and their email addresses. If the gift is a voucher or similar then they can send it to the recipient via email. If not, things will have to wait until we can finally see each other in person. But don't worry, we'll make sure we keep track of who should get what so we can remind people!
